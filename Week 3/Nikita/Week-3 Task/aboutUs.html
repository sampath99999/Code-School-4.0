Our Journey

We are a group of socially conscious tech innovators that share the same mission to transform the government space by finding smart solutions for big challenges. Our aim is to work on problems that affect at least a million lives through the government. Be it: building the country's finest Financial Management and Information System, or our work in traffic management and analytics, or building products in land survey to help pave the way for conclusive titleship; we are always striving to create the next breakthrough in government systems through powerful and transformative technology products.

At the end of the day, we are people who love what we do and there's nothing we'd be rather doing. We believe that all the hours we put in every day, all the innovation that happens in our workspace, every line of code that we write; is transforming lives even as of this moment.

As the great philosopher Federiech Neitzche said "the meaning of life is to live authentically and powerfully, creating one's own goals and values" and we find meaning through our goal of pioneering social change by helping implement good governance policies that better the nation.

Culture

Pixelvide is home to some of the most brilliant minds in the nation having graduated from top-tier universities for technology and management. Our pride emanates from people coming together from all over the country, to make a real-world impact. The youthful and vibrant atmosphere at Pixelvide is infectious and is the perfect base for the development of projects larger-than-life.
